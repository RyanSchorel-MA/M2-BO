Explanation: Whack a mole!

Whack-a-Mole is a classic game of chance in which the player uses a hammer to knock virtual moles out of the ground. whack-a-Mole. The moles appear randomly and for shorter and shorter periods of time on the surface. The player must hit the moles as quickly as possible before they disappear back into the ground. The more moles the player hits , the higher the score.
